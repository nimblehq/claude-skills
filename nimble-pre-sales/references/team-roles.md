## Core Roles

### Engineer (Developer)

Platform variants: **Web**, **iOS**, **Android**

Responsibilities:
- Write clean, maintainable, tested code
- Participate in technical decisions and code reviews
- Flag unclear requirements to PM/Team Lead
- Document code appropriately

When to include: Every project needs engineers. Select platforms based on project scope.

### Product Manager

Also covers Project Manager duties at Nimble.

Responsibilities:
- Define product vision, milestones, and user stories
- Manage schedule and deliverables
- Perform QA on staging before release
- Single point of contact for client communication
- Manage external stakeholders and third parties

When to include: Every project. One PM can typically handle a single squad.

### UX/UI Designer

Responsibilities:
- Deliver UX flows and UI designs per requirements
- Conduct market, competitor, and user research
- Maintain organized, accessible design source files
- Participate in design reviews

When to include: Projects with user-facing interfaces. Not needed for pure backend/API work.

## Senior Roles (Complex Projects)

### Solution Architect

A very senior Engineer responsible for overall technical direction.

Responsibilities:
- Define system architecture and technical standards
- Guide team on complex technical decisions
- Ensure consistency across components
- Evaluate build vs. buy decisions

When to include: Large, complex projects with multiple integrations, high scalability requirements, or significant technical risk. Not needed for straightforward builds.

### Program Manager

A very senior Product Manager for strategic oversight.

Responsibilities:
- Ensure product design and decisions are optimal
- Coordinate across multiple workstreams or parties
- Manage complex stakeholder relationships
- Strategic alignment and risk management

When to include: Multi-team engagements, projects with many external parties, or high-stakes strategic initiatives. Not needed for single-squad projects.

### Business Analyst

A Product Manager focused specifically on business analysis.

Responsibilities:
- Requirements gathering and documentation
- Process mapping and optimization
- Stakeholder interviews and synthesis
- Gap analysis and feasibility assessment

When to include: Enterprise clients who expect this role explicitly, or projects requiring heavy discovery and documentation before build. Nimble PMs are highly technical and can often cover BA duties.

## Team Sizing Guidance

| Project Type | Typical Team |
|--------------|--------------|
| Web app (single platform) | 1 PM, 1 Designer, 2-3 Web Engineers |
| Mobile app (iOS + Android) | 1 PM, 1 Designer, 2 iOS, 2 Android |
| Full platform (web + mobile) | 1 PM, 1 Designer, 2 Web, 2 iOS, 2 Android |
| Complex/enterprise | Add Solution Architect and/or Program Manager |
| Heavy discovery phase | Consider Business Analyst |

Adjust based on: timeline pressure, integration complexity, compliance requirements, client team capacity.
